## Why a commercial license?

Apache 2.0 is generous, but it requires that downstream users
preserve attribution and the NOTICE file, and it offers no warranty
or indemnification. Some organizations need:

- **No-attribution embedding** — ship valkyr inside a closed-source
  product without surfacing the Apache NOTICE / attribution to end
  users.
- **Indemnification** — a contractual commitment from the authors
  that valkyr does not infringe third-party IP, with defense and
  hold-harmless provisions.
- **SLA / support** — guaranteed response times, security-fix
  channels, custom-build support, training port access.
- **Custom terms** — bespoke terms for unusual deployment shapes
  (regulated industries, government, edge-device firmware, OEM
  inclusion).

The commercial license addresses all of these.
